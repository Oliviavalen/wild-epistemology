### The Creation Process of a Repo:

1.  Most of what I do in the human lifestyle systems I architect is abstract. I wanted a place where I could see all my pieces together on one “tangible” map.
2.  Then I figured: *great time to learn a new skill, so I put it on GitHub.*
3.  Then I thought: *since I’m building a playground with secret passages that connect all my systems together, I should invite others to play. And what makes playgrounds even more fun? Games, quests, and a story line.*
4.  Which led to the final realization: *with chronic loneliness at an all-time high, the best quest rewards are those that unlock deep, meaningful companionship.* So rewards are personal access to an actual human, namely me. *Don't worry, I'm an extrovert.* Authentic connections can lead anywhere. They're limited only by the imagination.

My mind has always been my playground, and now I’m inviting a few others to join. It’s designed to challenge your way of thinking about the world, hopefully optimize your personal life, create meaningful connections, and have fun. Like *Matrix* play?

Is the description a bit abstract? Yes. But if anyone can process the abstract, it’s you.
